Perfectly Grilled Steak

Description

"Savor the pinnacle of flavor with a succulent Michelin star steak, expertly grilled to perfection for an unparalleled culinary delight."

Ingredients

"Elevate your culinary skills: marinate prime steak, sear to perfection, and embrace the art of Michelin star steak grilling."

Ingredients List

  • 2 prime steak cuts (such as ribeye or filet mignon)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Remove the prime steak cuts from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature.
  2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, and fresh thyme leaves to create a marinade.
  3. Generously season the steaks with salt and freshly ground black pepper on both sides.
  4. Coat the steaks with the marinade, ensuring they are well covered. Let them marinate for about 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat the grill to high heat.
  6. Remove the steaks from the marinade and let excess marinade drip off.
  7. Place the steaks on the hot grill and sear for a few minutes on each side for the desired level of doneness.
  8. Use a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature. For medium-rare, aim for around 135°F (57°C).
  9. Remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes to allow juices to redistribute.
  10. Slice and serve the perfectly grilled Michelin star steaks with your favorite sides.
